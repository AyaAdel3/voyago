1. Introduction

Voyago ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information when you use our platform.

By using Voyago, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and profile photo when you register
  • Booking Information: Details entered when making hotel, restaurant, or tour guide reservations
  • Reviews & Ratings: Content you submit about services on the platform
  • Contact Messages: Messages sent through our Contact Us form
  • Chat History: Conversations with Emma (stored locally on your device)

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and screen size
  • IP Address: Used to understand general geographic location

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and confirm bookings and reservations
  • Personalize your experience and recommend relevant services
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send booking confirmations and important platform notifications
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in between sessions
  • Remember your preferences and saved plans
  • Store your chat history with Emma locally on your device
  • Analyze how users interact with the platform

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features.

5. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Hotels, restaurants, and tour guides when you make a booking — only the information necessary to complete your reservation
  • Technical Partners: Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements
  • AI Services: Chat messages sent to Emma are processed by our chatbot API to generate responses
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Chat history with Emma is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time from within the chat interface.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data
  • Regular security reviews of our platform

However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials private.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tawfik.mohamed.voyago@gmail.com

9. Children's Privacy

Voyago is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make significant changes, we will notify you via email or a notice on the platform. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.
